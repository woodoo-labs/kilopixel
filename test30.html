Reference Superpowers

Combining <pxl-group>, <pxl-layer>, and cross-element referencing to build tracking cameras, parallax depth, and dynamic visual filters.

1. The Tracking Camera

The player circle explores a giant cyberpunk grid. The camera <pxl-group> moves inversely to the player to keep them perfectly centered, zero JavaScript required.

2. Deep Parallax

Move your mouse. Multiple layers track the mouse distance with varying depth multipliers (0.05, 0.15, 0.3) to create true optical parallax over a deep space background.

3. Dynamic Scene Coloring

The "sun" shape moves across the sky. Its X coordinate natively drives the hsl() and hsla() color calculations for the sky gradient, the sun's glow, and the stroke highlights of every building in the city! Zero CSS filters, 100% mathematical reactivity at 60fps.
